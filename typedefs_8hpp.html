◆ DisplacementDirection

using DisplacementDirection = std::vector<std::pair<char,char> >

Vector for displacing eigenvectors the entries are pairs of the form (">";"x") where the first entry specifies the directions forward (">") ore backward ("<") of the derivative the second entry of each pair specifies the spatial direction of the derivative.

One can displace in "x", "y" or "z" direction.

Example: {(>;x),(<;y),(<;x),...,(>;z)}
